##VISION##
At its heart, this is a social insight game. A simulator as much as it is a story teller. It is about bringing believable relationships to life in a complex and real environment. Complete dragons with flaws, preferences, social status, histories, etc living withing a tribe withing a geopolitical landscape. This game should have the essence and feel of one of the Wings of Fire novels, with protagonist and antagonists and evolving character arcs. A clearly narrative based game with memorable quotes from all dragons involved, each character bringing a new and unique perspective. Memorable characters will developed and Players will create fondness and dislike for these characters which will , in turn, immerse them into the game of DranGen.
