sprite placement cheat sheet

Tail
self.body_tail_socket

Moves the red dot on the body.

self.tail_root

Moves which point inside the tail sprite tries to sit on that red dot.

self.tail_rotation_correction

Last-resort cleanup after rotation. Keep this near zero if possible.

Neck and head
self.body_neck_socket

Moves the cyan dot on the body.

self.neck_root

Moves which point in the neck sprite attaches to the cyan dot.

self.neck_head_socket

Moves the yellow dot on the neck.

self.head_root

Moves which point in the head sprite attaches to the yellow dot.

self.head_rotation_correction

Cleanup after head rotation.

+x → right
-y → up
